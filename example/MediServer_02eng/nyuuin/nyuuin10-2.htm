Visitors     
Visiting hours are limited in order to administer
treatment and give patients the rest they require.
Visiting hours are from noon to 8PM.
● Please refer to the “Visitor Regulations and Explanation of Medical Conditions” provided at the time of hospital admission.Please fill out the form and submit it.  
●

Visiting time may be limited further depending on the patient’s condition.

We appreciate your cooperation in this matter.

  
● If you are visiting the patient in a group, please use the floor lounge and avoid disturbing the other patients.
