3. You will be given an explanation about the effects and risks associated with your diagnosis, testing and treatment of the disease, as well as the availability of other treatment methods, how you are being cared for, and the course of the disease. ✤ We will provide the information needed to receive a second opinion, such as test results and treatment details upon request.
