1） Items All Patients Need
● Eating utensils： Mug, cup, teapot, insulated pot or water bottle, etc.
Easy drinking cup or bendable straw (if necessary).
    Chopsticks or spoons are readily available in the hospital,
but you can bring your own if you would like.
● Toiletries：

Toothbrush, toothpaste, cup, etc.
● Items needed for bathing： Soap, shampoo, rinse, basin, etc.
● Items needed to do laundry： Optional Items, laundry detergent, laundry net, laundry storage bag, etc.
● Items needed to use exercise equipment： Athletic shoes (please be sure to prepare athletic shoes for physical therapy)
