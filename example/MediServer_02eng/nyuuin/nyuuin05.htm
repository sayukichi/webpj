Ensuring Patient Safety

Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Ensuring Patient Safety
  At our hospital, we believe that cooperating with our patients and their family is essential to provide safe medical treatment.
We ask for your understanding and cooperation with the following matters.
  １. Preventing errors in the patient’s name and mistakes on the IV drip, oral medicine, tests, treatment, and blood transfusion.
● The patient’s identity should be confirmed by verifying their full name.
● The patient’s full name should be stated clearly to the medical staff.
 ●The patient’s written name should be verified whenever possible.
(intravenous drip or oral medicine, the name band given when admitted to the hospital, and blood type when a blood transfusion is required.)
