Hospital Discharge

1) Hospital Discharge Guidance   
● Please leave the hospital by 10AM on the day of hospital discharge.  
● We can provide an estimate (sum of expenses), if requested in advance.  
 
● You will be given an explanation about daily life after leaving the hospital.(The explanation will include how to use assistive tools and equipment, and precautions to take during everyday activities.)  
● For patients who are scheduled for a follow up appointment after discharge, we will let you know the next examination date and explain any necessary precautions.  
● For those who require it, we will explain about the Physical Disability Certificate and hospital rehabilitation.  
● We will return the patient registration card that you gave us at the time of admission.
※Please let a nurse know when you are able to leave the hospital room (return home).
