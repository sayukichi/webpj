3） Intraoperative Monitor Information  
  At our hospital, we take great effort to provide high-quality medical care and safe surgical procedures.
The explanation given about surgery should be sufficient to understand the procedure, but we have also prepared. an “intraoperative TV monitor” for everyone in the family to see the situation during the procedure.
If your family is interested, please contact your attending physician or nurse.
  ※Please note that you may not be allowed to view surgery depending on the method and site of the procedure.
