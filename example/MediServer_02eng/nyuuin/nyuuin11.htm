Hospital Bill Payment   

1) About Inpatient Medical Bill
Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Hospital Bill Payment
● Inpatient medical expenses are charged once a month.An invoice covering the amount until the end of the previous month will be delivered to your room on the 10th of the following month.  
● If you use a private room and a deluxe bed, the deluxe bed charge will be added to the basic hospitalization fee.  
  ※Deluxe bed fees are charged every 10 days.  
● Credit cards and debit cards are accepted for payment of hospital and deluxe bed fees.For details on how to use these payment methods, please ask the first floor reception desk.  
  ※There are financial institutions that cannot be used for debit card payments.
