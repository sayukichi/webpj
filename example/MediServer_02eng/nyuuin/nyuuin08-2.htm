Items Needed for Admission Procedures
● Please inform the reception desk if you had an injury or traffic accident while at work.  
● For on-the-job injuries (worker’s compensation insurance or an accident in the line of duty), a certificate issued by the employer or a relevant government office is required.  
● Long term care insurance card  
● Certificate of eligibility for ceiling-amount of personal-borne medical expenses (individuals who are exempted from Residence Tax payment: proof of reduction from the standard amount of expenses payable by inpatients).  
● Those who have been hospitalized in the past 3 months, please submit a certificate of hospital discharge  
● If you require any special applications, such as an application for social welfare, please inquire at the first floor reception desk as soon as possible.
