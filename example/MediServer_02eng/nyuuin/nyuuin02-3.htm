Meeting your expectations with ambition and skill.

   Nursing Department Policy  
1. We value the feelings of whoever we are with and face each other in good faith to demonstrate our full potential.
2. We will continue to learn in order to enhance our ability to respond appropriately to any situation.
3. We practice nursing by discussing and putting into practice the care we consider to be best, sharing our inspiration, and improving the quality of our services.
We have obtained the basic ward hospitalization rate 1 (7:1 hospitalization basic rate) at this hospital. Our nursing system introduces a team of regular nurses who are assigned to each individual patient. The night shift is carried out in two shifts.
