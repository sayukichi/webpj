CREDO(Creed)
● Social Medical Corporation Ryokusenkai uses a team approach to pursue expertise in medical care and welfare.Our most important mission is to provide the best medical technology and excellent service.
● We seek to satisfy our patients and their families from the bottom of our hearts, and we will maximize all the power we have and work hard to build your trust.
● We respect the human rights of each and every single patient, and the hospitality we share fills our hearts with gratitude.
By offering a warm healing space with rich sensibility, our greatest joy is making everyone smile with happiness.
