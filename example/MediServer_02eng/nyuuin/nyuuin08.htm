Hospital Admission Procedures

Items Needed for Admission Procedures
Admission procedures will take place at the first floor reception desk of Yonemori
Hospital on the day that you are admitted to the hospital.Please do not forget to
bring the following documents that are required for hospital admission.
● Patient registration card  
● Registered personal stamp (signatures are also accepted)  
● Health insurance card or late-stage medical care for the elderly insurance card  
Please also submit proof of any publicly financed health insurance
(social welfare or for a disease specified by the Japanese government as being
sworrisome and having no known treatment and of unknown causes).
