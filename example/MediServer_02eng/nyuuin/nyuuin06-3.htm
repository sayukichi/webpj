Before signing the consent form

Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Principles of Explanation and Consent
1. Please sign the agreement after noting that there are risks stated in the explanation and principles of consent.
2.

If you have any questions or concerns, please ask questions until you are satisfied with the response.


If you are not satisfied with the responses to your questions, do not make any hasty conclusions, we suggest that you ask another doctor for their opinion (second opinion).


We will provide the necessary materials.
Important and predictable complications and symptoms will be explained at the time of hospital admission and before surgical procedures.
3.

Patients are guaranteed the right to seek out a second opinion from another doctor.
Patients who seek out a second opinion will not be treated unfavorably as a result.
