About Consulting Doctors at Other Medical Institutions

Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  About Consulting Doctors at Other Medical Institutions
● In accordance with the diagnosis procedure combination (DPC) system, you will not be able to visit another medical institution during your stay at this hospital.
  If you believe it is absolutely necessary to consult another medical institution, please consult your attending physician or nurse.
※ During your hospital stay, if you admit yourself to another medical institution without consulting the staff at this hospital, you will be required to pay all of your medical expenses.
