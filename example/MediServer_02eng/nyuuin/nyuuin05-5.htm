  ６. Other

● Please help us ensure the safety of other hospitalized patients and medical staff and inform the staff if you notice any suspicious individuals.

● Please do not bring dangerous or unnecessary items to the hospital.

● Please note that we will follow hospital regulations if a member of our medical staff is threatened, insulted, or sexually harassed by a patient or a member of their family.
