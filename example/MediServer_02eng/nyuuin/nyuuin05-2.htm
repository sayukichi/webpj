  ２. Preventing risks such as falling
　The hospital environment is different from your living situation at home.
Falling accidents may occur due to the change in living environment, or illness and injuries that reduce physical strength and function. Unexpected falls and falling accidents are not rare in a hospital.
  Elderly patients who are advancing in age and losing cognitive abilities need to take extra precautions.
  The members of our medical staff are doing their best to keep our patients safe,
  But the attentiveness of the staff is not enough to completely eliminate the risk of falling.  We ask that our patients cooperate with the following.
● Please prepare and bring sleepwear and shoes that fit your body size.
● Please bring items that you require on a daily basis such as eyeglasses or
  a walking stick.
● We will ask for details about your everyday life when you are admitted to
  the hospital.We will use this information to arrange a suitable living situation
  to protect you from potential dangers in the hospital.
