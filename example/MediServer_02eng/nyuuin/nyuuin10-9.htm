About Disaster Prevention  
● Emergency exits and evacuation routes are explained by a member of our staff, but please double check this information by yourself when you are admitted to the hospital.
● There are fire doors, fire extinguishers, fire hydrants, smoke detectors, and sprinklers located on each floor.
In the event of a fire, please follow the instructions and guidance of the staff.
● In the event of an earthquake, protect yourself first and follow the instructions of the staff.
● Please do not use the elevator as it will be stopped in an emergency.
