Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Hospital Bill Payment
● Hospitalization fees are calculated on a per diem basis.  
● Hospitalization fees, medicine, injections, treatment, lab tests, and diagnostic imaging are included in the flat-rate portion of the bill.  
  ※Surgery and rehabilitation are calculated separately.  
● Those who are hospitalized should pay the bill within one week of receiving the invoice and those who will be discharged from the hospital should pay at the time of discharge.  
● Payment is handled at the reception desk on the first floor.  
  “Handling time” Monday - Saturday 8:30 am - 5:30 pm
