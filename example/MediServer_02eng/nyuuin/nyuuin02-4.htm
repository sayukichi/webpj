Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Charter of Patient Rights and Responsibilities
  Our hospital looks at health care from the patient’s point of view to provide reliable medical care that respects their human rights.
  Everyone enters healthcare with their own rights and responsibilities, and we would
like to support you by creating and pursuing collaborative medical services.
  Based on this idea, we established the “Patient Charter: Charter of Patient Rights and
Our Duties” as follows.
1. Regardless of the disease you have or circumstance you are in, 　you will be
respected as a human being, and you have the right to receive fair and high quality medical treatment without discrimination.
✤ We respect all of our patients as human beings and strive to provide fair and safe medical care.
