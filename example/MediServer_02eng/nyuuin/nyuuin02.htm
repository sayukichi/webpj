Hospital Philosophy

It is our pleasure to make everyone around us smile with happiness.

Fundamental Principles
● Guided by our commitment to our patients’ needs and satisfaction, we aim to provide advanced, high quality, and highly specialized orthopaedics and emergency medical care.  
● We make every effort possible to meet our patients’ needs by paying special attention to their well-being and providing detailed and easy-to-understand explanations.  
● By using harmony (in Japanese “和” or wa), we aim to create a visionary and pleasant work environment.
