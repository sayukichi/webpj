Going Out or Staying Out Overnight  
  Going out or staying overnight outside of the hospital
while you are hospitalized is not allowed.
  ※Please inform your attending physician or nurse as soon as possible.Please inform your attending physician or nurse as soon as possible.
  ※The hospital is not responsible for accidents or disasters that occur while you are out or staying overnight outside of the hospital.
  ※If you leave the hospital or stay outside of the hospital without acquiring permission, you will automatically be discharged.
