1） Items All Patients Need
● Please bring any medicine that was given to you by this hospital or any other hospital.(Including eye drops and ointments.)  
  Your regular doctor will not be able to prescribe medicine during your stay at this hospital.Please ask your regular doctor to prescribe more than enough of the medicine you need before you are admitted to the hospital.If you run out of medicine while you are hospitalized, please let a nurse know as soon as possible  
  Please write your name on all of your belongings.
※Please wash all towels and bath towels before bringing them with you.

 
● You may be asked to bring additional items depending on your condition and treatment.Details will be explained by the attending nurse.
