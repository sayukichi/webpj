Guide to Your Hospital Stay  >  Ensuring Patient Safety
  ３. Making a clear declaration of intention.
● Please make your intentions clear regarding blood transfusions if you have religious restrictions or a blood donor card.

● Priority is given to saving lives, but we will respect the patient’s intentions if they were made clear.
　If your personal intention is unclear, however, we will ask your family to make decisions on your behalf.
  ４. If you have any questions or concerns, please let us know.
● Please do not hesitate to ask a doctor or nurse if you have any concerns regarding your diagnosis, tests, treatment, or other matters.
